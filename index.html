About

Hello! I’m Jamie, a creative writing student based in Kent. I'm drawn to love in all its messy forms, poetry that lingers on small human moments, and the dark edges of dystopia. This site is a working public notebook — a mix of finished pieces and experiments side by side.

Interests: Poetry, dystopian themes, love and relationships, place-based writing, mental health, and other strange corners.
